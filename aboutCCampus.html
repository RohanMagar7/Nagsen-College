Nagsen Arts College Aurangabad

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar robustly believed that education was the only effective lever for the upliftment of the backward communities and downtrodden classes. Candidly speaking Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar was not only an educationist but he was also a great social revolutionary, a thinker, a philosopher and statesman, father of the Indian constitution and above all the apostle of social equality and lover of the poor.

About his great mission, Dr. B. R. Ambedkar said,"Whatever I have done I have been able to do after passing through frightful; crushing miseries and-endless troubles, all my life and fighting,with my opponents.

With great difficulty I have brought this carvan where it is seen today. Let the carvan march on despite the hurdles that may come its way.

If my Lieutenants are unable to take the Carvan ahead, they should leave it there, but under no circumstances should they allow the Carvan to go back. This is my message to my people.
